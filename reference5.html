About Elois Mbishibishi

I'm a crazy young boy learning to become a software developer. Growing up, I had no thought about going the computer science way; in fact, one of the scariest things for me was the "command line interface." Seeing some computer science geeks typing or reading things at the command line was really intimidating for me that I couldn't even believe myself doing things before that scary black screen someday. It wasn't until I questioned my fears and decided to step out of my confort zone and find out why so many computer scientist are confident and proud, that I found myself on the way to what I used to get me freak. And I am not yet done finding out why?
